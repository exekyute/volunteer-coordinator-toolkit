Shift Coverage Planner

Enter the shifts that need filling and the volunteers signed up for them. The page flags understaffed and overstaffed shifts and any volunteer double-booked across overlapping shifts. Only cleared volunteers count toward coverage. Assigning an uncleared volunteer is rejected and named, so a shift cannot be filled by someone who is not cleared.

Cleared roster

This is the roster produced by the Onboarding Eligibility Validator. Cleared volunteers can be scheduled. Blocked volunteers cannot. Load the cleared_roster.json from that tool to use a fresh roster.
