Onboarding Eligibility Validator

Check each volunteer's completed requirements before they are scheduled. The page flags an under-age volunteer, a missing or expired background check, any incomplete required training, and a missing or expired waiver. A volunteer is cleared only when every requirement is met. The cleared roster at the bottom is the same roster the Shift Coverage Planner uses.

Requirements in effect

Clearance is judged as of the as-of date. A background check or waiver counts as current when it is no older than its valid window on that date.
